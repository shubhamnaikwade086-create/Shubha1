UPSC Optional Subjects (Papers VI & VII)

The optional subject carries 500 marks (250 marks for each paper) and is a critical factor in determining the final rank. Candidates can choose one subject from a wide list of disciplines, including those from Arts, Science, Commerce, and Engineering.

The choice of optional is highly strategic, usually based on the candidate's academic background, interest, availability of study material, and the subject's performance history in the exam.

  • Botany
