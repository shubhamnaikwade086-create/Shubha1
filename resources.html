Essential Resources & Links for CSE

A well-defined set of resources is the foundation of a successful UPSC preparation strategy. Focus on quality, revision, and supplementing your core books with up-to-date current affairs.

Key Online References

  • **UPSC Official Website**: Mandatory for all official notifications, rules, and exam dates.
  • **NCERT Books**: The foundational books (Class VI to XII) for History, Geography, Polity, and Economics. Essential for basic clarity.
  • **Daily Current Affairs (The Hindu/Indian Express)**: Crucial for GS papers and the Interview. Daily reading is non-negotiable.
  • **Yojana & Kurukshetra Magazines**: Government publications that provide detailed analysis on socio-economic topics for Mains answer writing.

Study Material Recommendations

While subject-specific books vary, a typical resource strategy includes:

  • **Indian Polity:** M. Laxmikanth's *Indian Polity*
  • **Modern Indian History:** Bipan Chandra’s *India’s Struggle for Independence* or Spectrum Publications.
  • **Indian Economy:** Ramesh Singh’s *Indian Economy* or Sriram's notes, along with the latest Economic Survey and Budget.
  • **Environment:** Shankar IAS Academy Notes or PMF IAS material.
  • **Art & Culture:** Nitin Singhania's notes or NCERTs.
